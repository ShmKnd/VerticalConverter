# Privacy Policy — Vertical Converter

Last updated: March 15, 2026

## Overview

Vertical Converter is a macOS application that converts landscape videos 
to vertical format. This privacy policy explains how the app handles 
your data.

## Data Collection

Vertical Converter does not collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data or analytics are collected
- No crash reports are sent
- No telemetry of any kind is transmitted

## Internet Connection

Vertical Converter never connects to the internet. All video processing 
is performed entirely on your Mac. Your video files never leave your 
device.

## Local Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your Mac only:

- Your selected export settings (resolution, codec, bitrate, etc.)
- Your selected output folder preference

This information never leaves your Mac and is only used to restore 
your preferences between sessions.

## Third-Party Services

Vertical Converter does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics 
services, advertising networks, or cloud services.

## Children's Privacy

Vertical Converter does not collect data from anyone, including 
children under the age of 13.

If this policy changes in the future, the updated policy will be
